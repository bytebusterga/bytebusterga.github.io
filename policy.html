// Policies — Eastanollee, GA

Clear Rules.No Surprises.

Our policies are designed to protect both you and us. Please read through before booking a service or submitting a mail-in request.

01

Parts Ordering

A security deposit is required and must be paid upfront for all parts ordered. This deposit covers the full cost of parts and is non-refundable once parts have been ordered. Remaining labor and additional fees are due upon completion and must be paid prior to shipping for mail-in builds.

02

Mail-In Repairs

If you choose to mail in your device, please include a completed repair request form with your package. Repairs will begin only after both the package and form are received. Payment is required before the repaired device is shipped back to you.

03

Additional Policies

Estimates are provided prior to repair or build and are subject to change if additional issues are discovered during the process.
All custom orders are handled with care, but Byte Buster is not responsible for damages incurred during shipping unless shipping insurance is purchased.
Customers must provide accurate contact information to ensure timely communication throughout the repair or build process.
Any devices left unclaimed for more than 30 days become the property of Byte Buster.
