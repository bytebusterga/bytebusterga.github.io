Build Pricing

⚠️
Parts are always separate and paid upfront. Labor fees below cover assembly, cable management, OS install, and testing only. Parts cost is quoted and invoiced first — no parts are ordered without payment. All prices are estimates; a final quote is confirmed before any work begins.
How Labor Is Priced
I charge a labor range, not a flat fee — because no two builds are exactly the same. Unexpected issues, extra cable management, troubleshooting compatibility problems, or complex cooling setups all add time. The ranges below are honest estimates based on typical builds. I'll always confirm the final labor charge before shipping or pickup. You are never billed more than what was agreed to.
