My Story

Hey, I'm Anthony Gibson — the sole owner, operator, and technician behind Byte Buster, located at 300 Camp Rd in Eastanollee, GA. My journey started years ago with a dream to one day own a successful business. That drive came from watching my family work for themselves: my dad did masonry, my mom cleaned houses, and my uncle ran his own paint and auto body shop. Seeing them build something from nothing made me believe I could too.

I went to college to sharpen my skills and deepen my understanding of computer hardware and repair. I spent countless hours not just studying, but doing — fixing family PCs, completing console modding projects, and learning how to diagnose issues from the inside out.
